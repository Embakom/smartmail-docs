About SmartMail

SmartMail is a secure email platform that integrates with Gmail to help you send and receive emails safely. It scans all incoming and outgoing emails for malicious content and phishing links, classifies them, and stores suspicious emails in a secure trash box.

Features include:

  • Integration with Google Gmail accounts
  • End-to-end encryption for email communication
  • Malicious email detection and filtering
  • Automatic reporting of abnormal emails
